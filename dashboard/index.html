Data Analyst Portfolio Project

Urban Education & Mobility Analytics Dashboard

Comparative analysis of enrollment, student mobility, teacher compliance, and museum attendance trends across NYC and Chicago.

Project Goal

This project demonstrates analytical storytelling by combining education, mobility, onboarding, and cultural engagement metrics.

Understand student enrollment distribution in NYC
Analyze student movement trends in Chicago
Evaluate teacher onboarding performance
Compare museum engagement between cities

Interactive Filters

Explore the dashboard by year, city, borough, and school type.

Dataset Overview

This dashboard brings together multiple urban datasets to evaluate education and cultural engagement patterns.

NYC Higher Education Enrollment

Approx. 30,000 records used to evaluate enrollment distribution across boroughs and school types.

Chicago Student Mobility

Monthly movement records used to identify transfer trends and seasonal peaks.

Teacher Compliance Records

Onboarding records used to assess completion, delays, and escalation rates.

Museum Attendance Estimates

Cross-city museum visitor estimates used to compare cultural engagement in NYC and Chicago.
