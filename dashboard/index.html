Data Analyst Portfolio Project

Urban Education & Mobility Analytics Dashboard

A comparative analysis of education, mobility, onboarding, and museum attendance trends across NYC and Chicago.

Project Goal

Understand student enrollment distribution in NYC
Analyze student movement trends in Chicago
Evaluate teacher onboarding performance
Compare museum engagement between cities

Interactive Filters

Use filters to explore the dashboard by year, city, borough, and school type.

Dataset Overview

This dashboard combines multiple urban datasets to evaluate education and cultural engagement patterns.

NYC Higher Education Enrollment

Approx. 30,000 records used to evaluate enrollment distribution by borough and school type.

Chicago Student Mobility

Monthly movement data used to identify transfer trends and peak mobility periods.

Teacher Compliance Records

Onboarding and compliance records used to analyze completion performance and delays.

Museum Attendance Estimates

Cross-city visitor estimates used to compare cultural engagement between NYC and Chicago.

    Recommendations

    Analytical projects should end with decision-oriented takeaways.

    Increase onboarding monitoring to reduce teacher compliance delays.
    Investigate borough-level enrollment differences, especially Queens versus Manhattan.
    Prepare school operations for peak transfer periods in late spring.
    Expand museum promotion strategies in Chicago to reduce the attendance gap.
